Actions Guide

Short plain-English explanations for each action, with examples you can do today.

Share a verse with a friend Send a Bible verse to one person (text/DM/photo) and a one-line encouragement. Example: “John 3:16 encouraged me today—thinking of you.”
Serve Help at church, a neighbor, a school or club. Example: stack chairs after youth group; mow a yard; bring a meal.
Forgive Choose forgiveness over bitterness. Share (briefly) what changed for you—no names needed.
Family Life Do something intentional with your spouse/kids and say how it mattered (walk, game night, read a chapter).
Stand & Speak (Podium) A respectful public stand: short talk, testimony, signboard, or video speaking calmly about truth.
Invite to Church Invite 3 people. Mark if anyone came this week.
Share Christ (Evangelism) Share the gospel with someone today. Keep it kind and short; offer prayer.
Bridge Builder (Note/Call/Visit) Write a thank-you, reconcile, call a grandparent, visit someone lonely, encourage a missionary.
Public Testimony (Online Post) Post your testimony online—why Jesus changed your life—nominate 3 friends.
Film Night Host Host a Christian film night; discuss & pray together; post a group photo.
Favorite Charlie Video Share a Charlie clip and why it mattered to you.
Hands-Free Driver Commit to no phone handling while driving; post the pledge.
Family Table Focus Phone-free family meal streak (7 or 30 days). Post a table photo once in a while.
Quiet Time Focus 20+ minutes Bible/prayer streak (7 or 30 days). Share a brief insight.
Honor Service Thank a first responder, law enforcement, or military; pray for them. Post a thank-you.
Creator / YouTube Proceeds Donor Donate proceeds from a video/post/book/podcast; paste the donation receipt link when you Save & Count.
Phone Off at Church Put the phone away during worship; post the pledge.
Inspire Share a quote, story, or idea that pushed you to act today.

About “Like Charlie”

Like Charlie is a simple rhythm: do one small good thing, post it, invite 3 friends, and paste your link so it counts.

  • Step 1 — Choose an action and make your post.
  • Step 2 — Paste your post link and Save & Challenge.
  • Impact — See a growing, shared scoreboard (when global is enabled).

Donations and service are welcome. Claimed donations are marked unverified until approved to protect integrity.

Why it works

  • Small → Repeatable: tiny actions build momentum.
  • Public proof: a link to your post inspires others.
  • Invite 3: simple multiplication, not pressure.

How it works

Simple. Visible. Repeatable.

Copy‑paste caption for your first post

Today I’m starting the Charlie Kirk Challenge.

Charlie showed up boldly every day — here’s how I showed up boldly today:

📖 I chose the Bible, like Charlie.
I read Romans 8:28 — “And we know that all things work together for good to them that love God, to them who are the called according to his purpose.”
I shared it as encouragement to you.

❤️ I chose to forgive, like Charlie.
“To be a Christian means to forgive the inexcusable because God has forgiven the inexcusable in you.” — C.S. Lewis

👨‍👩‍👧 I chose life, like Charlie.
I spent intentional time with my younger kids magnet fishing — reminding them they are my greatest gift.

💵 I chose to pay it forward, like Charlie.
I donated to Turning Point USA.

🎤 I chose boldness, like Charlie.
I had a real conversation with someone I don’t agree with politically.

🌟 One of my favorite Charlie quotes:
“The Constitution doesn’t grant rights — it protects God-given rights.”

I challenge [@Friend1], [@Friend2], [@Friend3] to join me:
Share how you chose, like Charlie. Post your story/photo. Add your favorite Charlie quote. Donate to TPUSA (or your charity of choice). Nominate 3 more people.

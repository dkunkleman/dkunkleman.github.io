Privacy Policy

Effective: October 16, 2025

We built Live Like Charlie to make doing one small good action simple, shareable, and inspiring. This page explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control your information.

What we collect

  • Submission data: action name, platform, your post link, optional name/alias, optional screenshot URL, and timestamp.
  • Usage data: basic analytics such as pages visited and device type, used in aggregate to improve the site.
  • Local streaks: your streak badges are stored only on your device (browser localStorage).

We do not sell your data. We use aggregated, de-identified trends to improve the challenge.
